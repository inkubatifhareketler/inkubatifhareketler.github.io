About

Inkubatif Hareketler, founded by Emre Sokullu, is an incubator that aims to become a bridge between Silicon Valley early stage investment community and Turkish tech startups seizing the opportunity in AI, AR, VR, IoT and robotics (this list is subject to change as the tech landscape changes)

Not only we connect you with investors (Angel List can do that pretty well) but we also help you with the legal paperwork and mentor you through your early stages.

Inspired heavily by Y Combinator, here’s how we operate:

  • We have a single batch and two sessions every year. April session in Istanbul and October session in the US.
  • We pick 2-5 startups to invest for each batch.
  • Application deadlines are at March 15th.
  • We handle the legal paperwork for you, create your company in the US, pay about $3000-5000/founder, help with the visa, fly you over and host you in the US for about a month.
  • We continue mentoring you. We will be a resource to you with your next rounds.

For any questions, take a look at FAQ
