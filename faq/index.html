FAQ

Why is this website in English?

Because we believe if you can’t speak English you have zero chance succeeding in tech :-) All scientific paper and tech documentations are written in English.

Any limitations on the number of founders?

Nope. Y Combinator has a hard limit of 2+ (for a good reason) but we refrain ourselves from any kind of discrimination except your merits and credentials.

Any age limitations?

For the time being, we can only invest in and help people in their early 20s.

Domain limitations?

Tech that touches AI (articial intelligence) or AR (augemented reality) or VR (virtual reality) or robotics or better yet, a combination of these. Ventures with global ambitions.

How is this similar to Y Combinator?

Similarly to YC, we believe a small group of smart folks can change the world. We have a geographic focus on Turkey.

Are you a venture capital firm?

We are not. We’re hands-on (in the early stages) and focused on mentoring, yet we do put our money where our mouth is.

What do you get in return?

Similarly to YC, a certain percentage 5-15%

Why should I choose you over YC or any other combinator?

If you are based in Turkey, we have unique experience working in both countries and scaling a startup from 0 to 8 million startups. We believe we can help you better until you get to the Series A. After Series A, it’s no longer the incubator it’s the VC firm which backs you and connects you with more VCs – although we will still be active listen, coach and help you as a friend when you need it.
